Published by Grove Atlantic, this landmark book exposed the personalities, politics, and economics driving the contemporary art world. Featured on Charlie Rose Show and became essential reading for understanding the art market.

Major Work Art Criticism TV Appearance

Published by William Morrow & Co., this definitive chronicle of the legendary nightclub became a cultural phenomenon, providing insider access to the tornado of 1970s disco and drug excess.
