About Anthony Haden-Guest

Internationally known English-American writer, reporter, cartoonist, art critic, poet, and socialite who chronicles the intersections of art, culture, and society from New York City and London.

Born in Paris in 1937, Anthony has spent decades documenting the cultural landscape through his writing and visual art. His work has appeared in major publications including Vanity Fair, The New Yorker, The Paris Review, and Esquire.
