About the Artist

Anthony Haden-Guest brings decades of cultural observation to his cartoon work, combining sharp wit with keen social insight.

As an Emmy Award-winning writer and cultural critic, Haden-Guest has documented the intersections of art, society, and human behavior for major publications including Vanity Fair, The New Yorker, and The Paris Review.

His cartoon series represent a visual extension of his literary work, offering satirical commentary on contemporary life with the same perceptive eye that has made him one of culture's most distinctive voices.
